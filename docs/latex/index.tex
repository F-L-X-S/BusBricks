Object-\/oriented building-\/blocks to customize bus-\/communication for different services and interfaces on Arduino-\/\+MCUs\hypertarget{index_autotoc_md1}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Vision}{Vision}}\label{index_autotoc_md1}
Provide configurable software-\/modules to customize the communication between services, hosted on different MCUs. ~\newline
 Bus\+Bricks are templates to derive classes for mapping service-\/specific rules to different bus-\/systems. The communication between the hosting devices is therefore separated from the inter-\/service-\/communication. That means, the communication between the services can follow it\textquotesingle{}s own (from the chosen communication-\/interface independent) rules. ~\newline

\hypertarget{index_autotoc_md4}{}\doxysubsection{\texorpdfstring{Service-\/ and Communication-\/\+Layer}{Service-\/ and Communication-\/\+Layer}}\label{index_autotoc_md4}
The separation of inter-\/service-\/ and inter-\/mcu-\/communication-\/rules requires a separation of the software modules. ~\newline
~\newline
\hypertarget{index_autotoc_md5}{}\doxysubsubsubsection{\texorpdfstring{Service-\/\+Layer}{Service-\/\+Layer}}\label{index_autotoc_md5}
{\bfseries{Services}} are defined in the {\bfseries{service-\/layer}}. All implementations in the service-\/layer are Service-\/specific and therefore they should not be dependent the chosen communication-\/interface. That means, the service-\/layer-\/modules should be {\bfseries{reusable, independent which communication-\/interface the host-\/device supports}}.\hypertarget{index_autotoc_md6}{}\doxysubsubsubsection{\texorpdfstring{Communication-\/\+Layer}{Communication-\/\+Layer}}\label{index_autotoc_md6}
Modules being part of the Communication-\/\+Layer are specific for the chosen bus (e.\+g. I2C, One\+Wire, Musbus-\/rtu...) the host device provides. They are responsible for {\bfseries{applying the bus-\/protocol-\/specific rules (e.\+g. CRC-\/check and -\/calculation, communication-\/timeouts, framelength...) to the content (PDU)}} provided by the services. ~\newline
\hypertarget{index_autotoc_md7}{}\doxysubsection{\texorpdfstring{Content and representation}{Content and representation}}\label{index_autotoc_md7}
The cascading of processing information and the rules applied to it lead to the concept of {\itshape content-\/ and representation}.~\newline
 In every iteration, an information is processed and the rules of the next level, closer to the physical layer are applied to it, the information closer to the format the service is able to process is called {\bfseries{\doxylink{class_content}{Content}}}.~\newline
 Conversely, the format, the information has after applying the rules of the next level closer to "{}\+Layer-\/0"{} is called {\bfseries{representation}}.~\newline
